Dasara

Directed by: Srikanth Odela

Starring: Nani, Keerthy Suresh

Release Date: March 30, 2023

Synopsis

Veerlapally, a small village surrounded by coal mines, is a place where men can't survive without alcohol. Dharni, who loves Vennela, sacrifices his love when he finds out Vennela loves Suri. Years later, Dharni and Suri, who steal coal for a living, face conflicts involving a bar job and village politics, leading to tragic consequences.
